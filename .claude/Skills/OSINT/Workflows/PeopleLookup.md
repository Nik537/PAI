# People Lookup Workflow

## Ethical Pre-Check
Before proceeding, confirm:
- Legitimate purpose for the research
- Subject is a public figure or research is authorized
- Compliance with applicable laws

## Phase 1: Identity Verification
- Full name variations
- Known aliases
- Age/DOB range
- Location history

## Phase 2: Professional Presence
- LinkedIn profile
- Professional websites
- Company affiliations
- Conference appearances

## Phase 3: Social Media
- Twitter/X
- Facebook (public)
- Instagram (public)
- Other platforms

## Phase 4: Public Records
- Business filings
- Court records (if relevant)
- Property records
- Professional licenses

## Phase 5: Media Presence
- News mentions
- Interviews
- Podcasts
- Published works

## Phase 6: Network Analysis
- Known associates
- Professional connections
- Organizational memberships

## Phase 7: Synthesis
- Compile findings
- Assess confidence levels
- Identify gaps
- Generate report
