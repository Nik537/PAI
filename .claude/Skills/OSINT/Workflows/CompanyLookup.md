# Company Lookup Workflow

## Phase 1: Corporate Identity
- Legal name and DBA
- Business registration
- Incorporation details
- Registered agents

## Phase 2: Leadership & Ownership
- Executive team
- Board members
- Major shareholders
- Beneficial owners

## Phase 3: Financial Profile
- Public filings (if applicable)
- Funding history
- Revenue estimates
- Financial health indicators

## Phase 4: Operations
- Headquarters and offices
- Employee count
- Products/services
- Tech stack

## Phase 5: Market Position
- Competitors
- Market share
- Customer base
- Partnerships

## Phase 6: Reputation
- News coverage
- Reviews (Glassdoor, G2)
- Legal issues
- Regulatory compliance

## Phase 7: Synthesis
- SWOT summary
- Risk assessment
- Key findings report
