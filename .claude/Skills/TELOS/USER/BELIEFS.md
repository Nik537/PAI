## Core Beliefs About Life

### Everything is a Skill
- Social skills, confidence, attractiveness, emotional intelligence - all can be developed through deliberate practice
- No artificial limitations, only growth opportunities

### Take 100% Responsibility
- No blaming others for your situation
- No victim mentality
- Your response to any situation is always your choice

### Focus Only on What You Can Control
- Things you can control: thoughts, words, actions, habits
- Things you can influence: relationships, some environment
- Things outside control: others' opinions, the past, most external events
- Only invest emotional energy in what you can control

### You Are What You Consume
- Content diet shapes the mind
- 80% intentional/growth-oriented, 20% entertainment
- Curate inputs carefully
