# My Mission

*Your life mission and purpose. What drives you?*

## Core Purpose

To continuously improve myself across all dimensions - physical, mental, emotional, social - and share that journey to help others do the same.

## Vision

**The Complete Man:** Someone who has developed competence across life domains, maintains authentic confidence, has clear identity and boundaries, and lives with purpose while not taking himself too seriously.

**Success looks like:**
- Physical health optimized (fitness, nutrition, biohacking)
- Mental clarity and emotional regulation mastered
- Meaningful relationships built on honest communication
- Content/knowledge shared that helps others transform
- Financial freedom through value creation
- Living authentically, expressing truth even when uncomfortable

## Values

### Primary Values (What guides decisions)

1. **Health** - Physical and mental wellness as the foundation
2. **Growth** - Continuous self-improvement, everything is learnable
3. **Authenticity** - Truth over comfort, express real self
4. **Clarity** - Peace of mind, understanding self and situation
5. **Responsibility** - Own your life, no victim mentality
6. **Connection** - Deep relationships over superficial ones
7. **Contribution** - Share knowledge, help others grow

### Operating Principles

- **Identity drives behavior:** See yourself as someone committed to growth
- **Displacement over deprivation:** Fill life with good things, bad habits fall away
- **Focus on controllables:** Only invest energy in what you can change
- **Skill mindset:** Everything can be learned and improved
- **Persistence:** Keep working until done, iterate to success
