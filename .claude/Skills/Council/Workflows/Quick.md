# Quick Council Workflow

## Process

### Step 1: Frame Question
Clarify the topic for quick perspectives.

### Step 2: Gather Perspectives
In a single round, have each council member provide their key take (1 paragraph each).

### Step 3: Summarize
Present all perspectives with a brief synthesis of key tensions and alignments.

## Output Format

```markdown
# Quick Council: [Topic]

**Optimist:** [Brief take]
**Skeptic:** [Brief take]
**Pragmatist:** [Brief take]
**Visionary:** [Brief take]
**Historian:** [Brief take]

## Key Tensions
- [Tension 1]

## Recommendation
[Brief synthesis]
```
